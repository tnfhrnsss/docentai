Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Is DocentAI free? â–¼
Currently in development stage, the Chrome Extension is provided free of charge. Premium plans based on API usage may be added in the future.
Q. Does it support platforms other than Netflix? â–¼
Currently supports Netflix only, with plans to expand to YouTube, Disney+, Wavve, and more.
Q. Is my privacy protected? â–¼
DocentAI only sends video metadata (title, episode, subtitle text) and does not collect user identification information. All data is transmitted encrypted.
Q. Is AI analysis always accurate? â–¼
Using Google Gemini 3 Flash provides high accuracy, but occasional errors may occur. We continuously improve through user feedback.
Q. Can I use it offline? â–¼
Internet connection is required for AI analysis. However, previously cached explanations can be viewed offline.
Q. How does the 2-step architecture work? â–¼
Step 1 (Video Registration): When you start watching, DocentAI runs Search Grounding once to gather background knowledge about the show. Results are cached in the database.

Step 2 (Explanations): When you request explanations, the AI uses the cached references instead of searching again. This makes responses faster (25ms for cached) and more cost-effective.

This intelligent caching is why DocentAI can provide instant, contextual explanations without expensive repeated searches.
Q. What makes this different from a simple AI wrapper? â–¼
